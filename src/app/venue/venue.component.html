Medieval Abbey Buildings, Abingdon

All the festivites on the day will be happening at the Medieval Abbey Buildings in Abingdon town centre.

"The beautiful medieval buildings are more than 600 years old, set in their own private walled gardens and with a mill stream running alongside. The buildings are a scheduled ancient monument, once part of Abingdon’s famous Abbey, and are full of history and unique in character. The Abbey Buildings are a combination of the Abbey Buildings: The Long Gallery and The Unicorn Theatre."

Experience Oxfordshire - Abingdon Abbey Buildings
The ceremony will be taking place at the Unicorn Theatre at the Medieval Abbey Buildings, which is also known as The Unicorn Cinema.
